About me

I'm a Solution Engineer at Esri, where I help organizations unlock the power of geospatial technology — from enterprise GIS deployments to custom web applications built on ArcGIS.

Outside the day job, I'm drawn to two things: writing long-form pieces that untangle complex ideas, and making maps that go beyond utility to become something genuinely worth looking at.

The thread connecting all of it is a belief that technical work and storytelling aren't opposites. The best maps have a point of view. The best documentation reads like prose. I try to bring that sensibility to everything I make.
